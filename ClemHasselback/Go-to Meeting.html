For voice inputs, you can use one of 2 options (You pick which option by choosing the appropriate radio button): telephone or Input-Mic.

telephone - you will see a phone number to call along with 2 numbers to be entered in. You would call the specified telephone number and enter in the 2 numbers when prompted from the telephone. You would call the number after you connect to the meeting

Input-Mic - Please use "ear-buds" or something equivalent if you use a computer Input Microphone. Otherwise, we get feedback echos. The input device can be built in or an add-on to your computer. If you use this option, your voice would be transmitted across the internet.
